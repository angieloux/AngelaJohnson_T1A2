About Me

Experience

Well, hello! Thanks for dropping in. I'm Angela Johnson--AJ for short.

Born and bred in Brisbane, now living in Melbourne.

I'm currently undergoing a Full-Stack Web Development course at Coder Academy, working towards my Diploma of Information Technology.

I recieved my Bachelor Degree in Agribusiness from UQ in 2015, worked in corporate agribusiness finance for 4 years, before travelling the world. The journey I took opened my eyes to the possibilities that this big blue dot really holds. I landed a job running operations for a YC-backed fintech startup in San Francisco, helping immigrants access financial freedom in the US. This journey told me I needed to be one of the decision makers, one of the builders -- that actually brought about real-world change and effected peoples lives in a tangible way. That's when I applied to Coder Academy.

As a passionate philomath, I'm excited to continue to learn so I can find my niche in this industry.
