About Me

Experience

Well, hello! Thanks for dropping in. I'm Angela Johnson. But you can call me AJ. I'm a Melbourne-based aspiring web developer, currently completing my Diploma of IT in full-stack web development at Coder Academy.

After receiving my Bachelor's Degree in Agribusiness 2015 (UQ), I worked in corporate agribusiness finance for 4 years, before travelling the world.

The journey I took opened my eyes to the possibilities that this big blue dot really holds. I landed a job running operations for a YC-backed fintech startup in San Francisco, helping immigrants access financial freedom in the US.

I realised then, I wanted to one of the builders -- that actually brought about real-world change and affected peoples lives in a tangible way. That's when I applied to Coder Academy.

As a passionate philomath, I'm excited to continue to learn so I can find my niche in this industry.
